## 📊 Test Categories

### 🎯 Major Tests (Comprehensive)

#### **🏆 Full Syllabus Coverage**
- **Duration**: 3 hours (180 minutes)
- **Format**: Complete exam simulation
- **Coverage**: All subjects and topics
- **Purpose**: Final preparation and assessment
- **Frequency**: Monthly major assessments

#### **📈 Major Test Features**
- **Complete Pattern**: Exact exam format replication
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: All syllabus areas
- **Time Management**: Realistic exam conditions
- **Performance Analytics**: Detailed score breakdown
- **Comparative Analysis**: Rank and percentile data

---

### ⚡ Minor Tests (Targeted Practice)

#### **🎯 Focused Topic Assessment**
- **Duration**: 1-2 hours
- **Format**: Topic-specific or section-wise
- **Coverage**: Targeted subject areas
- **Purpose**: Continuous monitoring and improvement
- **Frequency**: Weekly minor assessments

#### **📚 Minor Test Benefits**
- **Regular Practice**: Consistent skill development
- **Topic Mastery**: Focused area improvement
- **Quick Feedback**: Immediate performance insights
- **Confidence Building**: Progressive skill building
- **Weakness Identification**: Early problem detection

## 🎪 Test Preparation Strategy

### 🌟 Optimal Test Utilization

> [!tip] Strategic Test Approach
> 
> **📅 Pre-Test Preparation**
> - Review relevant syllabus areas
> - Practice time management techniques
> - Prepare mentally for test conditions
> - Organize study materials and notes
> 
> **⏰ During Test**
> - Maintain strict time discipline
> - Follow systematic question approach
> - Mark difficult questions for review
> - Manage stress and maintain focus
> 
> **📊 Post-Test Analysis**
> - Detailed performance review
> - Identify specific knowledge gaps
> - Plan focused improvement areas
> - Schedule follow-up study sessions
