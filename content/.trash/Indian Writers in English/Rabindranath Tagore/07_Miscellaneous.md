# Miscellaneous

*   **Contemporaries:** W.B. Yeats, Ezra Pound, and other modernist writers.
*   **Influence:** Tagore had a profound influence on Indian literature and art. He is also considered a major figure in the Bengal Renaissance.
*   **Interesting Fact:** Tagore was also a talented painter and composer. He composed over 2,000 songs, which are now known as Rabindra Sangeet.
