# Gitanjali (Song Offerings)

*   **Plot Summary:** *Gitanjali* is a collection of 103 prose poems, which are Tagore's own English translations of his Bengali poems. The poems are primarily devotional, with a strong spiritual and philosophical bent. They explore themes of love, life, death, and the divine.
*   **Major Themes:** The central theme of *Gitanjali* is the realization of the divine through love and devotion. Other important themes include the relationship between humanity and nature, the transient nature of life, and the search for spiritual enlightenment.
*   **Literary Style:** The poems are written in a simple, lyrical, and musical style. Tagore uses a variety of poetic devices, including metaphors, similes, and personification, to create a rich and evocative imagery.
