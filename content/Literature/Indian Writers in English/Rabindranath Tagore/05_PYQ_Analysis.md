# PYQ Analysis

Based on the analysis of previous year questions, the following areas are most frequently tested:

*   **Major Works:** Questions are often asked about the authorship of his major works, especially *Gitanjali*, *The Home and the World*, and *The Post Office*.
*   **Awards and Recognition:** The Nobel Prize in Literature is a recurring topic.
*   **Themes:** Questions may be asked about the major themes in his works, such as spirituality, nationalism, and nature.
*   **Characters:** Questions may be asked about the main characters in his novels, such as Bimala, Nikhil, and Sandip in *The Home and the World*.
