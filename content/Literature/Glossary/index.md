## 🎭 Literary Movements & Periods

### ⏰ Historical Context Terms

#### **Renaissance & Early Modern**
- **[[Renaissance]]**: Cultural and intellectual rebirth
- **[[Humanism]]**: Human-centered philosophy
- **[[University Wits]]**: Elizabethan dramatists
- **[[Euphuism]]**: Ornate prose style

#### **17th-18th Century** 
- **[[Jacobean Age]]**: Post-Elizabethan literature
- **[[Metaphysical Conceits]]**: Complex poetic imagery
- **[[Heroic Couplet]]**: Rhyming verse form
- **[[Scholasticism]]**: Medieval academic method

#### **Romantic & Victorian**
- **[[Byronic Hero]]**: Rebellious literary character type
