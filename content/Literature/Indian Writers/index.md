## 🎭 Literary Generations

### 📖 Historical Development

#### **🌅 Pioneer Generation (Early 20th Century)**
- **[[Rabindra Nath Tagore/index|Rabindranath Tagore]]**: International recognition, cultural bridge
- **[[Sarojini Naidu/index|Sarojini Naidu]]**: The Nightingale of India
- **[[Toru Dutt/index|Toru Dutt]]**: Early Indo-English poetry

#### **🌱 Foundation Builders (1930s-1950s)**  
- **[[Mulk Raj Anand/index|Mulk Raj Anand]]**: Social realism and reform
- **[[R.K. Narayan/index|R.K. Narayan]]**: Indian middle-class storytelling
- **Raja Rao**: Philosophical fiction

#### **🌸 Modern Voices (1960s-1980s)**
- **[[Kamala Das/index|Kamala Das]]**: Confessional poetry and feminism
- **[[Anita Desai/index|Anita Desai]]**: Psychological complexity
- **[[Nissim Ezekiel/index|Nissim Ezekiel]]**: Urban Indian experience

#### **🌟 Contemporary Masters (1990s-Present)**
- **[[Arundhati Roy/index|Arundhati Roy]]**: Magical realism and activism
- **[[Amitav Ghosh/index|Amitav Ghosh]]**: Historical fiction and diaspora
- **[[Shashi Deshpande/index|Shashi Deshpande]]**: Women's experiences
