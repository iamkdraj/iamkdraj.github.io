# 2. Works and Awards of Ruskin Bond

This section provides a comprehensive catalog of Ruskin Bond's major works across various genres and the significant awards and honors he has received for his contributions to literature, especially for children.

## Major Works

Ruskin Bond is a prolific writer, having authored hundreds of short stories, essays, novels, and books for children. His works often reflect his love for nature and the simple life in the Indian hills.

### Novels

*   **_The Room on the Roof_** (1956) - His first novel, a semi-autobiographical story of Rusty.
*   **_Vagrants in the Valley_** (Sequel to *The Room on the Roof*)
*   **_The Blue Umbrella_** (1974) - A short novel for children, adapted into a film.
*   **_A Flight of Pigeons_** (1978) - A historical novella set during the 1857 Indian Rebellion.
*   **_Delhi Is Not Far_** (1994) - About the dreams of a struggling writer, Arun.
*   **_The Hidden Pool_** (1966) - His first novel for children.

### Short Story Collections

*   **_Our Trees Still Grow in Dehra_** (1992) - Won the Sahitya Akademi Award. A collection of 14 interconnected short stories.
*   **_The Night Train at Deoli and Other Stories_** (A collection of 30 short stories)
*   **_Time Stops at Shamli and Other Stories_** (A collection of 21 stories)
*   **_A Season of Ghosts_** (Includes 9 short stories and one novella)
*   **_The India I Love_** (A collection of 19 heartfelt poems and essays)

### Children's Books

Ruskin Bond is particularly beloved for his children's literature.

*   **_The Blue Umbrella_** (1974)
*   **_Grandfather's Private Zoo_**
*   **_Angry River Ghost Trouble_**
*   **_Cricket for the Crocodile_**
*   **_Rusty, The Boy from the Hills_** (A series of stories about his alter ego, Rusty)
*   **_Children's Omnibus_** (A collection of some of his best-loved stories)

### Non-Fiction / Memoirs / Essays

*   **_Roads to Mussoorie_** (2005) - An ode to his home in Mussoorie.
*   **_Rain in the Mountains_** (A collection of stories, snippets, essays, and poems)
*   **_A Book of Simple Living_** (A personal diary)
*   **_Looking for the Rainbow_** (2017) - Part of his memoir series.
*   **_Till the Clouds Roll By_** (2017) - Part of his memoir series.
*   **_Coming Round the Mountain_** (2019) - Part of his memoir series.
*   **_A Song of India_** (2020) - Fourth installment in his memoir series.
*   **_Scenes from a Writer's Life_**
*   **_The Lamp is Lit_**

### Other Notable Works

*   **_A Face in the Dark and Other Hauntings_** (A collection of paranormal tales)
*   **_Susanna's Seven Husbands_** (A short story adapted into the film *7 Khoon Maaf*)

## Awards and Honors

*   **Sahitya Akademi Award (1992):** For his short story collection *Our Trees Still Grow in Dehra*.
*   **Padma Shri (1999):** India's fourth-highest civilian award, for his contributions to children's literature.
*   **Padma Bhushan (2014):** India's third-highest civilian award.
*   **National Film Award for Best Children's Film (2007):** For the film adaptation of *The Blue Umbrella*.
