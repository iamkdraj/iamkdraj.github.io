# 2. Works and Awards of Anita Desai

This section provides a comprehensive catalog of Anita Desai's major works across various genres and the significant awards and honors she has received for her contributions to literature.

## Major Works

Anita Desai is primarily known for her novels, which delve into the psychological complexities and inner lives of her characters. She has also written short stories and children's books.

### Novels

*   **_Cry, the Peacock_** (1963) - Her debut novel, focusing on the neurotic heroine Maya.
*   **_Voices in the City_** (1965) - Explores the lives of a Bengali family in Calcutta, particularly Monisha.
*   **_Bye-Bye Blackbird_** (1971) - Deals with the experiences of Indian immigrants in England.
*   **_Where Shall We Go This Summer?_** (1975) - The story of Sita, a woman grappling with disillusionment.
*   **_Fire on the Mountain_** (1977) - Won the Sahitya Akademi Award. Centers on three women: Nanda Kaul, Rekha, and Ila Das.
*   **_Clear Light of Day_** (1980) - Shortlisted for the Booker Prize. Focuses on family relationships and the passage of time.
*   **_The Village by the Sea_** (1982) - A children's novel that won the Guardian Children's Fiction Prize.
*   **_In Custody_** (1984) - Shortlisted for the Booker Prize. Explores the world of Urdu poetry and a struggling academic.
*   **_Baumgartner's Bombay_** (1988) - Recounts the tragic life of a German Jew in India.
*   **_Journey to Ithaca_** (1995) - Explores a couple's spiritual quest in India.
*   **_Fasting, Feasting_** (1999) - Shortlisted for the Booker Prize. Contrasts life in India with life in America.
*   **_Diamond Dust and Other Stories_** (2000) - A collection of short stories.
*   **_The Artist of Disappearance_** (2011) - A collection of three novellas.
*   **_The Zigzag Way_** (2004) - Set in 20th-century Mexico.

### Short Story Collections

*   **_Games at Twilight and Other Stories_** (1978)
*   **_Diamond Dust and Other Stories_** (2000)

### Children's Books

*   **_The Village by the Sea_** (1982)
*   **_The Peacock Garden_** (1974)
*   **_Cat on a Houseboat_** (1976)

## Awards and Honors

*   **Sahitya Akademi Award (1978):** For her novel *Fire on the Mountain*.
*   **Guardian Children's Fiction Prize (1983):** For her children's novel *The Village by the Sea*.
*   **Booker Prize Shortlist:**
    *   *Clear Light of Day* (1980)
    *   *In Custody* (1984)
    *   *Fasting, Feasting* (1999)
*   **Literary Lion Award (1993)**
*   **Padma Bhushan (2014):** India's third-highest civilian award.
*   **Honorary Fellow of the University of Cambridge**
