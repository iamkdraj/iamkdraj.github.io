Pre-university preparation materials focusing on advanced literature analysis and critical thinking skills.

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

### 🌟 Key Skills Development

- **Literary Analysis**: Deep understanding of themes, symbols, and literary devices
- **Critical Thinking**: Analyzing text within historical and cultural contexts
- **Writing Skills**: Developing analytical and creative writing abilities
- **Cultural Awareness**: Understanding diverse perspectives through literature

---

## 📚 Study Approach

### 🔍 Analysis Framework

> [!important] Literary Analysis Steps
> 1. **First Reading**: Understand the basic plot/theme
> 2. **Deep Analysis**: Identify literary devices, symbols, themes
> 3. **Contextual Study**: Historical and biographical context
> 4. **Critical Response**: Form personal interpretations and responses

---

## 🎨 Themes & Concepts

### 📝 Major Literary Themes

- **Human Relationships**: Parent-child dynamics in "My Mother At Sixty Six"
- **Cultural Identity**: Language and belonging in "The Last Lesson" 
- **Time & Memory**: How past and present intertwine
- **Loss & Acceptance**: Dealing with change and mortality
